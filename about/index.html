About

I currently hold the following technical qualifications

  • SUSE Certified Administrator (SCA) in Enterprise Linux 11 & 12
  • SUSE Certified Engineer (SCE) in Enterprise Linux 11 & 12
  • SUSE Certified Administrator (SCA) in OpenStack Cloud
  • OpenStack Foundation Certified OpenStack Administrator (COA)

and have previously held the following

  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
  • Cisco Certified Academy Instructor (CCAI)

In 2009 I was nominated and selected to become a Novell Knowledge Partner and I have been reselected since then, now as a Knowledge Partner for SUSE and Micro Focus (who acquired the Novell and SUSE brands via The Attachmate Group).
